# **App Name**: FarmersBuddy

## Core Features:

- Climate Modelling: AI-driven analysis of historical and real-time weather data to suggest optimal crops, sowing times, and irrigation schedules.
- Disease Management: Image analysis tool to detect plant diseases from uploaded leaf images, providing severity levels and treatment suggestions.
- Soil Testing Integration: Analysis of soil pH, nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels to recommend suitable crops, fertilizers, and organic alternatives.
- Market Forecasting: AI-driven prediction of future crop price trends and comparison tool. Suggests the best time to sell and displays nearby market rates.
- Crop Listings: Allow buyers to view live crop listings with quality indicators and compare prices.
- Direct Messaging: Enable direct communication between farmers and buyers.
- Multilingual Voice Assistance: Provide voice-activated assistance in multiple languages for hands-free operation and easy access to information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#6B8E23) to reflect agriculture and nature.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a clean and natural feel.
- Accent color: Warm gold (#D4A27A) to highlight key information and calls to action.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif font for readability in various languages. Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', using 'PT Sans' for the body of longer text
- Use clear and recognizable icons related to farming, weather, and market trends.
- Implement a simple and clean interface with large icons and buttons for ease of use, especially on small screens.
- Use subtle animations, via Lottie, for interactive elements like loading states, transitions, and confirmations.
